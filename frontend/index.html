How It Works

Two types of users, one simple platform. Companies post, applicants apply.

01
🏢 Company Registers

Up to 5 companies can register on InternHub. Each gets their own private dashboard to manage roles and review applications.

02
📋 Post Internship Roles

Companies post the internship roles they're offering — title, domain, location, duration, stipend, required skills, and description.

03
🎓 Applicants Apply

Applicants register, browse all open roles from all companies, and submit their application with skills and resume summary.

04
🔍 Company Reviews

Companies see all applications for their own roles on their dashboard. They can shortlist, reject, or mark applicants as selected.

05
📊 Track Status

Applicants can see the status of every application they've submitted — Pending, Shortlisted, or Rejected — in real time.

06
💸 Completely Free

No subscriptions, no payment gateways. InternHub is a free, open-source platform you can deploy yourself in minutes.
